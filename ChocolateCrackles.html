CHOCOLATE CRACKLES


Ingredients:

- 4 cups Rice Bubbles
- 1 cup icing sugar
- 1 cup desiccated coconut
- 250g copha, chopped
- 3 Tbsp cocoa







Step 1




In a large bowl, mix the Rice Bubbles, icing sugar, cocoa & coconut.

















Step 2



Slowly melt the copha in a saucepan over a low heat. Allow to cool slightly. Add to Rice Bubbles mixture, stirring until well combined.








Step 3




Spoon mixture into paper patty cases and refrigerate until firm. Finally enjoy.
