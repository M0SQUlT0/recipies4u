TACOS


Ingredients:
- 1kg Beef Mince
- 2 Tablespoons Tomato Paste
- 1 Tablespoon Beef Stock
- 500 Milliliters Boiling Water
- 2 Tablespoon Worcestershire Sauce
- 12 Tortilla Breads

Step 1



Pre-heat an oven to 175℃. Put a sauce pan on a stove and have it on high heat, with your spoon seperate the meat pieces to let it cook faster.

Step 2



Once the meat is almost cooked, pour out the fat oil into a bin, put the tomato paste and the worcestershire sauce in the sauce pan and get the boiling water, mix it with the Beef stock and put it into the sauce pan.
